This is a crowdsourced effort to map inundated roads in Baton Rouge. We rely on users to maintain the accuracy of flood reports. Please do your part, especially once roads have dried out.


Zoom in to your street and click/tap to report it as flooded or cleared. If you accidentally mark a street, just click/tap again to undo the change.


All data are open. Big thanks to OpenStreetMap India for the code!
